About the Platform

MedWatchPro is an advanced AI-driven drug safety intelligence system. By integrating molecular chemistry, protein structures, and large-scale knowledge graphs, it provides researchers and clinicians with deep insights into adverse drug reactions, polypharmacy risks, and genetic biomarker responses — all fully explainable through attention mechanisms.

01 Architecture & Models

Multi-Modal Molecular Encoding

We fuse state-of-the-art transformer models for structural biology. ChemBERTa processes SMILES strings into 384-dimensional molecular embeddings, while ProtBERT translates protein sequences into robust 1024-dimensional continuous representations. These modalities are unified into a shared latent space.

Heterogeneous Graph Transformer (HGT)

At the core of the engine sits a massive 4-node, 5-edge-type knowledge graph indexing 14,616 drugs, 5,237 proteins, 144 biomarkers, and 100 severe side effects. The data is processed via a 2-layer HGTConv network featuring multi-head attention.

02 Clinical Capabilities

Zero-Shot Novel Compound Prediction

The system is not limited to known drugs. By analyzing the raw SMILES string of any novel or experimental compound, MedWatchPro can embed the molecule live, inject it into the existing graph framework, and instantly predict potential clinical risks without any retraining required.

Polypharmacy Risk Analysis

Analyzing a single drug is rarely enough. By aggregating embeddings across combinations of 2 to 3 drugs simultaneously, the system computes synergy scores. These scores highlight safety risks where adverse effects are amplified beyond the sum of their individual parts.

Temporal Onset Prediction

Leveraging over 1.14 million real-world adverse event reports from the FDA FAERS database, MedWatchPro moves beyond binary risk flags by predicting the clinical onset window for side effects, classifying reactions dynamically from acute to chronic.

Pharmacogenomics Biomarkers

A specialized dual-decoder jointly predicts 100 side effects while simultaneously classifying 144 key genetic biomarkers as either adverse, efficacy-related, or other, all in a single forward pass.

03 Trust & Transparency

Counterfactual Explainability (XAI)

Medical AI requires trust. Our system utilizes a custom modified HGTConv architecture to capture self-attention weights directly from the graph message-passing layers. This reveals precisely which protein targets and drug-drug interactions drove the model toward a specific predictive outcome.
